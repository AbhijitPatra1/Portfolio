  • Electric Standee Scooter

    EAGLE, CATIA

    India's electric vehicle revolution is gaining momentum, but dependence on imported Electronic Control Units (ECUs) is a major hurdle. That's where my project, 'Powertrain Design and EV,' comes in! We tackled this challenge by developing our own ECU for an electric scooter prototype. We used a BLDC motor and Li-iron battery for efficiency and sustainability, and even crafted a unique wooden-metallic frame for optimal performance. The best part? Building this prototype without relying on imported components! This project demonstrates my expertise in powertrain design, ECU development, and my passion for overcoming import dependencies within India's burgeoning EV sector.

  • Over Votage Over Current Protection Circuit

    Diod,UA741 Comparator,Transformer,Capacitor,IC17812,Resister,Zener Diode,Transister,Vero Board

    Imagine a power supply circuit that could malfunction and send dangerous voltage spikes to delicate electronics. This project utilizes a low-cost comparator circuit to detect these spikes and instantly shut down the supply, preventing damage and ensuring user safety.The core of my project lies in the UA741 comparator, a versatile and affordable IC. It continuously compares the voltage or current level of the circuit with a predetermined reference value. If the input exceeds the threshold, the comparator triggers an alarm or shuts down the circuit, preventing component damage and potential safety hazards.
